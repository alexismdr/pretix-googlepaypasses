{% blocktrans trimmed %} Please be aware, that contrary to other virtual wallets/passes (like Apple Wallet), Google Pay Passes are not handled offline. Every pass that is created, has to be transmitted to Google Inc.

By clicking the "Save to phone"-button below, we will transfer some of your personal information, which is necessary to provide you with your Google Pay Pass, to Google Inc.

Please be aware, that there is no way to delete the data, once it has been transmitted.

However we will anonymize all passes that are not linked to a device on a regular, best effort basis. While this will remove your personal information from the pass, we cannot guarantee that Google is not keeping a history of the previous passes. {% endblocktrans %}
